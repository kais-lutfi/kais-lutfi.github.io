What did you learn about yourself?

Even though the bootcamp hasn't and the job isn't done, it wasn't easy getting through to this stage. I learned that it's possible for me to achieve what I need to with hard work and determination. I'm also aware that I need to try to improve my emotional intelligence to acheive better things in my career and life.

Role of Values, Empathy, and Self-awareness

Those characteristics are important regardless of where you are in the journey of life. In terms of learning and becoming a programmer, it is those traits that will guide you and help make you a better person.

What surprised me?

Neuroplasticity and the growth mindset. It is fascinating to think that you can still mend your brain for the better.

Challenges

The most challenging part for me was trying to regularly meditate. I managed to do sometimes and I always felt like a better human being afterwards. I'll try to do it as much as I can!

Why core learning?

Other than helping us becoming better humans, help us become better student, better future employees!

Waste of time?

Not at all! I enjoy those parts and its always calming reflecting back and having deep thoughts. We need to be good people to achieve good things afterall!
